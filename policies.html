PLEASE READ THOROUGHLY BEFORE BOOKING. ALL MY POLICIES ARE STRICLY ENFORCED!

Late Policy

There is a 15 mins grace period, after 15 mins late, you will be charged a $15 LATE FEE.

  • If you are more than 20 minutes late, your appointment will be CANCELED and you will be CHARGED 50% of the total price of the service.
  • If you NO-SHOW/ NO CALL you will be charged the remainder of your service and will never be able to book with me again :/

Deposite Policy

Your appointment is NOT confirmed until you make a NON- refundable deposit of $35 at the time of your booking.

  • The deposit must be made WITHIN 2 hours of your booking otherwise your appointment will be CANCELED.
  • Final payments can be made with a card or cash. (If the remaining amount is paid with a card there will be a small fee added to your final amount.)

Cancelation Policy

If you cancel WITHIN the same day of your appointment you WILL BE CHARGED a 50% fee. Please make sure to cancel at least 24 hours prior to your appointment.

  • The non-refundable deposit is TRANSFERABLE to a new appointment made WITHIN 7 DAYS, only if you cancel 24 hours prior to your appointment.

Lash With A Friend Policy

Bring a friend with you to get your full lash set at a DISCOUNTED price. You must come in at the SAME time as your appointment with your friend. You are able to leave once I finish lashing whoever goes first (you don’t have to wait for your friend to be done with her set as well.)

  • The final amount must be paid in one transaction (cannot be split between 2)

Model's Policy

If you are interested in being a model and getting your lash set at no cost, please email or text me the set you would want to be done. PLEASE DO NOT BOOK until I confirm with you. I am only taking 12 models for the month of October/ November. 3 models for each set (first comes first served.)
